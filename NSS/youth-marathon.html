Youth Marathon

Finishing a marathon is a state of mind that says anything is possible

To promote the wellness of our body and mind Jiit ,sector 128 organized JIIT youth marathon on the first day of converge i.e. on 2nd Feb, 2019 inside Jaypee Greens like every other year. The theme for the marathon was to support girl child and spread awareness. We were proud to have so many event partners who supported our cause. Some of the major sponsors supporting this cause were Decathlon, Ruby enterprises, Madhusudan and Vrapup.

The event started early morning at 8:30 am and our respected director Dr. Hari Om Gupta graced the occasion with his presence and kind words accompanied by distinguished faculty coordinators. It was an inter college and open for all event witnessing participants from different colleges. Apart from students staff members also participated enthusiastically.

Top 3 Winners were rewarded by our title sponsor enrico eyewear accompanied by gifts from bonn, Madhusudan Chaach, Vrapup hampers and decathlon Vouchers. Our top 10 winners were also acknowledged by our associate sponsors.

  • Date: 2 February, 2019 | 8:30AM - 9:30AM
  • Location: Jaypee Institute of Information and Technology, Sector 128, Noida
