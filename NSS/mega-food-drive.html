Possit nostro aeterno eu vis, ut cum quem reque

We make a living by what we get, but we make a life by what we give. Keeping this motto as a vision in our mind, on 15 th august 2018 a Mega Food Drive was conducted by Robin Hood Army under National Service Scheme. The day was started by gathering volunteers in the college bus and collecting leftover food from restaurants which included yellow dal and rice. The main motive was to distribute the food to various clusters in sector 62 and 63. Many students and teachers of Jaypee Institute Of Information Technology Sector-128 participated in the drive. Teams were formed according to the number of clusters and the food was distributed among people. All the volunteers were excited and enjoy working their task. The work was appreciated by all the people and they showed their gratitude towards the initiative. Everything went well and the day ended with all the students and teachers gaining new experience and learning new things. When asked to one of the volunteers about the best part of the day, the answer was, “To see others are smiling because of you is the best feeling of the world”.
