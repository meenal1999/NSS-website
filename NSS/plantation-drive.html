Plantation Drive

Plant a Tree, Grow a Flower- Let’s give mother earth to give them back her power. Plants eliminate the pollution and are beneficial for the health, too. This would let our coming generations lead a healthy life. An obsession with industrial progress has caused man to lose sight of the tenuous ecological balance. The consequences of mindless deforestation are clear now. 

Keeping these in mind, the volunteers of National Service Scheme of Jaypee Institute of information technology sec-128 conducted a plantation drive on 21st February 2019 in the government school of Asgarpur.

The day started with collecting different plant saplings, spades and necessary equipments, buying snacks for the school children and then gathered in the College bus. The volunteers as well as the school children were equally excited for the upcoming day. Volunteers were greeted with a very warm welcome.

About the statistics, there were around 33 saplings planted which included fruit trees like mango, guava and flowerering plants like mogra, bougainvillea and hibiscus. Everything was done from scratch, from dugging earth to planting the saplings and watering them as well. There was also interaction with the students, keeping them busy by playing different games like "antakshari" and came to know some pretty good singers.

After the plantation, volunteers provided the children with snacks and played "kho kho" with them. It was a bliss spreading happiness and spending time with them. All the happy faces were captured in frame and the gleaming heart in memory. This is how the blissful day of plantation drive ended and it was a massive success, measuring by the happiness spread to mother Nature as well as her children.
