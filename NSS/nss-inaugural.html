Possit nostro aeterno eu vis, ut cum quem reque

“Not me but you”. The National Service Scheme (NSS) is a voluntary and value - based student youth program successfully launched on Saturday, 12 January 2019 at the Jaypee Institute of Information Technology, Noida. There are many times when we are powerless and wonder why no one does anything about the welfare of society. The Ministry of Youth Affairs and Sports founded NSS for one of these reasons. This is a two-year program under which JIIT students voluntarily serve the community and raise social awareness among students. The NSS Inaugural event was held in the Multi-Purpose Hall of JIIT, Sector 128 campus to establish links between "Campus and Community," "College and Village" and "Knowledge and Action." The event began with a warm welcome address, followed by the Lighting of the Lamp. The audience for the ceremony included all the volunteers from both campuses (sector 128 and 62), our honorary dignitaries, professors and students from government schools who all joined the NSS song. Vice Chancellor Prof. S. C. Saxena gave a very heartfelt and awakening speech about NSS and how we should be proud participants. Next, our own Dean, Prof. Hariom Gupta, addressed the audience and wished us luck. At last, Prof. Alka Sharma guided us on the NSS program. There were amazing dance performances by participants from both campuses, followed by a spectacular performance by Fortissimo-The Music Society of the Sector -128. Last but not least Abhivayakti-The Dramatic Society performed the incredible play Vish-Was, which was all about the blind faith prominent in society. Finally, the Inaugural ceremony came to an end with the National Anthem, Snacks and the Sampling plantation ceremony. The opening ceremony of the NSS was complemented by drawing competitions for students of government schools and an enriching and fun workshop for volunteers. The competition was judged by Prof. Alka Sharma and the 1st winner was given a bag, the 2nd a paint box and subsequent winners were gifted lunchbox and water bottles. It was a wonderful competition in which the students performed extremely well. Finally, an enriching and fun workshop was organized for volunteers.
