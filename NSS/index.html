Events We Organised

We organised several events previous year, reaching rural areas to raise awareness regarding several concerns.

Holi Celebration

  • 21 March, 2019 | 12:00PM - 3:00PM
  • Middle Govt School Asgarpur Village Noida

Many students from the college went to a nearby school to play holi with the underprivileged children. Faces were masked with colour relaxing the social rules.

Blood donation event

  • 14th March 2019
  • Jaypee Institute of Information and Technology, Sector 128, Noida

Keeping the noble thought of saving lives in mind, Jaypee Institute of Information Technology Sector 128 in association with Red Army and AIIMS CNC Blood Bank under the aegis of NSS scheme organised a blood donation camp on 14th March 2019 from 10 a.m. to 4 p.m in multipurpose hall of JIIT.

Flash Mob

  • Saturday, 11th August 2018
  • G.I.P Mall Noida Sector-18

Flash Mob is a type of cultural event wherein the various societies of our college showcase their talents to support a cause. On Saturday, 11th August 2018, a Health Camp and a Flash Mob were held to spread awareness about women health issues and the benefits of regular checkups.

Youth Marathon

  • 2 February, 2019 | 8:30AM - 9:30AM
  • Jaypee Institute of Information and Technology, Sector 128, Noida

To promote the wellness of our body and mind Jiit ,sector 128 organized JIIT youth marathon on the first day of converge i.e. on 2nd Feb, 2019 inside Jaypee Greens like every other year. The theme for the marathon was to support girl child and spread awareness.

Mega Food Drive

  • 24 October, 2018 | 8:00AM - 11:00PM
  • Village Clusters in Noida Sector-62,63

We make a living by what we get, but we make a life by what we give. Keeping this motto as a vision in our mind, on 15 th august 2018 a Mega Food Drive was conducted by Robin Hood Army under National Service Scheme.

Nss Inaurgal Ceremany

  • 12 January, 2019 | 9:00AM - 5:00PM
  • Jaypee Institute of Information and Technology, Sector 128, Noida

The National Service Scheme (NSS) is a voluntary and value - based student youth program successfully launched on Saturday, 12 January 2019 at the Jaypee Institute of Information Technology, Noida.

Plantation Drive

  • 21 February, 2019 | 12:00PM - 4:00PM
  • Middle Govt School Asgarpur Village Noida

Plant a Tree, Grow a Flower- Let’s give mother earth to give them back her power. Plants eliminate the pollution and are beneficial for the health, too. This would let our coming generations lead a healthy life. An obsession with industrial progress has caused man to lose sight of the tenuous ecological balance. The consequences of mindless deforestation are clear now. 

Vegan Outreach Program

  • 19 February, 2019 | 10:00AM - 4:00PM
  • Jaypee Institute of Information and Technology, Sector 128, Noida

To promote Veganism, a practice of abstaining from the use of animal product particularly in diet and an associated philosophy that rejects the commodity status of an animal, the NGO “Vegan Outreach” in association with Jaypee Institute of Information and Technology, Sector 128, Noida (UP) organized a publicizing campaign on 19 February 2019, from 10 a.m. to 4 p.m. at the college campus itself.

Health Camp

  • 15 August, 2018 | 8:00AM - 5:00PM
  • Noida Sector-77

Good health and good sense are two of life’s greatest blessings, they are not only responsible for calm mind but also bring inner strength and self confidence. Understanding the importance of having good health, respected professors and the volunteers of JIIT sector-62 and sector-128 joined hands with Robinhood army so as to detect health issues and intervene in time.
